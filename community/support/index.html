    Support¶

      While this module is not supported by VMware Support Services, it is supported by the project maintainers and its community of users.

      Use the GitHub issues to report bugs or suggest enhancements.

      Issues are monitored by the maintainers and are prioritized based on criticality and community reactions.

      Before opening an issue, please search the issues and use the reactions to add votes to matching issues. Please include as much information as you can. Details like these are incredibly useful in helping the us evaluate and prioritize any changes:

    • A reproducible test case or series of steps.
    • Any modifications you've made relevant to the bug.
    • Anything unusual about your environment or deployment.

      You can also start a discussion on the GitHub discussions area to ask questions or share ideas.
